RFQ checklist

The most accurate quotes come from clear requirements. Use this checklist to reduce assumptions and speed technical-commercial alignment.

Include these items when available

  • Current drawing revision and referenced standards/specifications
  • BoM details and any approved alternatives
  • Required quantities, release profile, and timeline
  • Verification criteria, acceptance requirements, and evidence needs
  • Packaging, handling, and delivery constraints
  • Traceability scope (material certs, lot control, build records)
  • First Article requirements (if required by contract)
  • NDA status and secure file-sharing preference

Secure document transfer

For drawings, technical data packs, and sensitive programme documents, use a secure upload workflow rather than plain email attachments.

  • Step 1: Request a secure upload link from our team
  • Step 2: Receive a time-limited link and reference ID
  • Step 3: Upload files and confirm your submission by email
  • Optional: For highly sensitive files, use AES-256 encrypted archive and share password separately by phone
