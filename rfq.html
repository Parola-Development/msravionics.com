RFQ checklist

This checklist helps us quote accurately and reduces rework caused by missing spec details. Use it as a template.

Send these items (as applicable)

  • Drawing(s) with revision and any referenced specifications
  • Bill of materials (BoM) and approved alternatives (if any)
  • Quantities and delivery schedule (including lifetime volume expectations)
  • Test/verification requirements and acceptance criteria
  • Packaging/handling requirements
  • Traceability expectations (material certs, lot control, build records)
  • First Article requirements (if applicable)
  • NDA status (in place / required)
